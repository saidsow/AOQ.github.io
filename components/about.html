Matt Aptaker

Matt Aptaker is a dedicated sports professional with a passion for helping athletes achieve their full potential. With a background in sports management and marketing, Matt brings a wealth of experience to the AOQ Sports team.

Matt has worked with numerous athletes and sports organizations, providing strategic guidance and support to help them reach their goals. His expertise in branding and promotion has been instrumental in elevating the profiles of many athletes.

At AOQ Sports, Matt is responsible for overseeing the development and execution of marketing campaigns, ensuring that our clients receive the best possible exposure. He is committed to building strong relationships with our clients and helping them succeed both on and off the field.

Artak "The Armenian Nightmare" Petrosyan

Artak "The Armenian Nightmare," Petrosyan, is a retired professional mixed martial artist and a former Colorado Combat Club Champion. Competing in the welterweight division. With over 15 years in the sport, he is a seasoned MMA veteran who holds a black belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu from Gracie Barra.

Artak began his professional career in regional promotions, quickly making a mark with his dynamic fighting style and resilience in the cage. He secured the Colorado Combat Club Welterweight Championship, and built a reputation as a fierce competitor who always left it all in the cage.

Now retired from active competition, Artak has transitioned his passion for MMA into management and mentorship. Leveraging his years of experience, he is committed to guiding the next generation of fighters, helping them navigate their careers, and achieve success both inside and outside the cage.
