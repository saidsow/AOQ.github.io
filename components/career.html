Ilima-Lei Macfarlane

MMA Fighter | Former Bellator Flyweight Champion

Ilima-Lei "The Ilimanator" Macfarlane is a professional mixed martial artist and the inaugural and former Bellator MMA Women's Flyweight World Champion. Born and raised in Hawaii, Ilima-Lei has established herself as one of the most dominant champions in women's MMA, with an impressive professional record and a reputation for exciting finishes.

Beyond her fighting career, Ilima-Lei is deeply committed to cultural advocacy and youth development in her native Hawaii, where she runs programs to empower young Hawaiian women through martial arts.

@ilimanator

Said Sowma

Professional MMA Fighter | Heavyweight Division

Said Sowma is a professional mixed martial artist competing in the Heavyweight division. With his impressive size, athleticism, and technical skill, Said has established himself as a formidable competitor in the heavyweight ranks.

Known for his well-rounded game and evolving skillset, Said continues to develop as a fighter and test himself against elite competition. His dedication to the sport and professional demeanor make him an excellent representative both in and outside of the cage.

@saidyokohama

Kevin Croom

MMA Fighter | "The Hard-Hitting Hillbilly"

Kevin "The Hard-Hitting Hillbilly" Croom is a veteran mixed martial artist with experience across multiple major promotions. With over 30 professional fights to his name, Kevin has built a reputation as an exciting fighter who never backs down from a challenge.

Known for his relentless style and finishing ability, Kevin has competed around the world and continues to showcase his evolving skillset and warrior spirit every time he steps into the cage. Outside of fighting, Kevin is committed to giving back to his community through youth coaching and mentorship.

@kevincroom_mma

Julius Anglickas

MMA Fighter | Light Heavyweight

Julius Anglickas is a Lithuanian-born mixed martial artist competing in the light heavyweight division. A former LFA Light Heavyweight Champion, Julius has showcased his impressive grappling and ever-improving striking on the big stage, challenging for the Bellator Light Heavyweight World Championship.

With a background in wrestling and a dedication to constant improvement, Julius continues to climb the ranks in one of MMA's most storied divisions. His work ethic and professional approach to the sport make him both a formidable competitor and an ideal representative for sponsors.

@julius.anglickas

Grant Neal

MMA Fighter | Light Heavyweight Division

Grant Neal is an undefeated mixed martial artist with a background as a collegiate football player and All-American wrestler. His exceptional athleticism and physical strength have translated perfectly to MMA, where he has dominated competition with his powerful wrestling and improving striking skills.

With his disciplined approach to training and competition, Grant continues to develop into a more complete fighter with each performance. His combination of elite athleticism, technical skill, and marketable personality makes him a rising star to watch in the light heavyweight division.

@grant_neal

Tyrell Fortune

MMA Fighter | Heavyweight Division

Tyrell Fortune is an American mixed martial artist and accomplished wrestler competing in the heavyweight division. A NCAA Division II National Champion and World Team Member in freestyle wrestling, Tyrell brings elite athleticism and grappling credentials to the cage.

Since transitioning to MMA, Tyrell has demonstrated his ability to dominate opponents with his wrestling while developing a well-rounded striking game. His combination of high-level athleticism, technical skill, and marketable personality makes him one of the most promising heavyweights in the sport today.

@tyrellfortune
